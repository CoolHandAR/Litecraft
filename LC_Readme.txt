Inputs:
W = FORWARD
S = BACKWARDS
A = STRAFE_LEFT
D = STRAFE_RIGHT
MOUSE_UP_DOWN = CYCLE THROUGH HOTBAR
0-9 = HOTBAR SLOTS
I = OPEN INVENTORY
TAB = CONSOLE
P = OPEN RENDERER PANEL
F6 = TOGGLE FULLSCREEN
F = TOGGLE FREEFLY

Inventory
Open with I then, select on a desired block and press 0-9 to assign to a slot

Use cvar lc_static_world = 0 in the console, to generate world actively or 1 to keep it static
